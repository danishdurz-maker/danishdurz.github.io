        </article>
        <article class="card">

      <h2>Task 3 · Impact &amp; Analysis</h2>
      <div class="grid grid-2">
        <div class="card">
          <h3>Advantages</h3>

            <li><strong>Drones:</strong> Safer inspections, faster progress tracking, accurate as-builts.</li>
            <li><strong>Digital Twin:</strong> Lower energy usage and optimised building performance.</li>
            <li><strong>Predictive Maintenance:</strong> Reduced downtime and smarter asset management.</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
        <div class="card">
          <h3>Challenges</h3>
          <ul class="list">
            <li>High setup cost (software, sensors, hardware).</li>

            <li>Training needs &amp; industry readiness.</li>
            <li>Cybersecurity concerns in IoT systems.</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
